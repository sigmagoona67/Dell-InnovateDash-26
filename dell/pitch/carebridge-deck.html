Problem Statement

How might we use technology to help youth workers efficiently summarize, manage, and guide conversations with multiple youths — while giving youths a seamless, empathetic after-hours experience — so workers can deliver timely, personalized follow-ups?

CareBridge AI02
The Problem

Care happens during the day. Distress happens at night.

Workers juggle many youths

A single youth worker holds a large caseload. Keeping each story, history, and next step in mind is slow and easy to lose.

Signals are scattered

Notes, chats, and check-ins live in different places. Important warning signs sit unread between sessions.

After-hours distress is seen too late

Youth reach out when they are alone at night — but help only arrives the next working day, after the moment has passed.

The result: youth feel unheard in their hardest hours, and workers walk into each morning without a clear, prioritized picture of who needs them most.

CareBridge AI05
What Is CareBridge AI

One platform, two portals — a bridge between them.

Youth Portal warm · companion · after-hours

A calm, low-arousal space where youth do a quick mood check-in and chat with an empathetic AI companion when no one else is awake — designed to feel safe, not clinical.

Staff Portal operational · triage · daytime

A caseload dashboard where youth workers get AI-generated summaries, risk alerts, case insights, and compatibility-based matching — so the next day's follow-ups are timely and personalized.

The bridge

AI summarizes and screens the overnight conversation, then routes it to the right worker for human follow-up.

CareBridge AI09
How Risk Is Read

Calm, not alarming — clarity without panic.

High risk

Solid danger fill + accent bar. Surfaced at the top of the caseload for immediate human attention.

MediumLow

Soft chips. Read by weight, shape, and label — distinct from operational status, never by color alone.

Operational status

Unassigned · Onboarding · Offline · Incomplete use a separate violet family — so "medium risk" never collides with "offline".

Every signal stays low-arousal and trustworthy. The product reassures vulnerable youth and keeps workers calm and decisive.
